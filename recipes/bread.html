Brioche Bread

Description

Bread is a baked food product made from water, flour, and often yeast. It is a staple food across the world, particularly in Europe and the Middle East. Throughout recorded history and around the world, it has been an important part of many cultures' diets.

ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Honey Or Sugar
  • Milk and Eggs

Steps

  1. Activate Yeast:Dissolve yeast in a warm milk with a pinch of sugar until foamy
  2. Mix Dough:Combine flour, sugar, and salt in a mixer. Add eggs and the yeast mixture; mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Knead:Knead with a dough hook for 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Add Butter:Gradually add softened butter, piece by piece, kneading until fully incorporated and the dough is smooth and shiny.
  5. First Rise:Cover and proof in a warm place until doubled (1-2 hours).
  6. Chill:Deflate the dough, cover, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. ShapeShape the cold dough as desired (loaf, rolls, braid) and place in a greased pan.
  8. Second Rise:Cover and proof in a warm place until puffy and nearly doubled (1-1.5 hours).
  9. Bake: Brush with egg wash and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35-45 minutes until golden brown.
  10. CoolLet cool in the pan, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely before slicing.
